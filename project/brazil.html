São Paulo, Brazil

The biggest city in South America

Sao Paulo is the largest city in Latin America, offering a variety of things to do. It's a metropolis with grand buildings and natural landscapes.

Weather

São Paulo has a subtropical climate characterized by warm, humid, and rainy summers and short, cool winters. The best time to visit the city is generally considered to be from April to September. During these months, rainfall is much lower, temperatures are more pleasant, and the weather is drier. You can immerse yourself in its urban culture, its parks, museums, and the myriad cultural offerings that São Paulo offers.

São Paulo's Culinary...

I recommend you try the mortadella bread sold at the Sao Paulo Municipal Market; it's simply incredible. Also, many pizzas are sold in Sao Paulo every day, so you could enjoy a Paulista pizza very soon...
