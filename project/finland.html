Tallinn, one of the best-preserved medieval cities in Europe...

Tallinn is a beautiful and ancient city in Estonia. Its most special part is the Old Town, one of the best-preserved medieval cities in Europe. The streets are made of ancient cobblestones, and you can see colorful houses, tall church towers, and ancient walls. The city has a magical atmosphere. You can visit the main square, admire the ancient walls, or simply get lost in its small, beautiful streets. This is truly an unforgettable place.

Beyond its medieval charm, Tallinn is also a modern city with vibrant cafes, innovative museums, and a lively arts scene.
The city’s seaside promenades offer stunning views of the Baltic Sea, and its parks provide peaceful spots to relax.
Whether you are interested in history, culture, or simply enjoying beautiful scenery, Tallinn has something for everyone.
