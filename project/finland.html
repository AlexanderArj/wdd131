Helsinki, Finland

The Capital of Finland

Helsinki is known for its stunning architecture, particularly its grand cathedrals and historical squares. The city offers a modern design and rich heritage, with its location on the shores of the Baltic Sea. You can take a short walk from the famous Helsinki Cathedral to the bustling Market Square and the main port, where ferries depart to nearby islands.

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Helsinki is between late May and early September, when the days are long and temperatures are mild, perfect for exploring on foot.

Helsinki: the city of great cathedrals...
