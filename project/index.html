About me

My name is Alexander, and I'm a passionate traveler, a programming student, and an amateur musician. Perhaps influenced by my mother, I've always been curious about exploring the world and immersing myself in its diverse cultures. Recently, I've been fortunate enough to travel to some beautiful cities, from the bustling streets of São Paulo, Brazil, to the tranquil landscapes of Kadriorg in Estonia.

This website is the result of my desire to share those experiences with you. Every corner of the planet has its own story, and in this space, I'm dedicated to unraveling some of the most important of those secrets. My greatest satisfaction would be knowing that this page has helped you plan an unforgettable trip and discover the magic that lies beyond your comfort zone.
