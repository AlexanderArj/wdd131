Barranquilla, Colombia

The golden gate of Colombia

Barranquilla is a very beautiful city in Colombia that receives many tourists annually, especially during the Barranquilla Carnival, a popular festival recognized by UNESCO.

Weather

Being a coastal city, it's generally very hot, but there are sometimes very rainy days as well. The weather is somewhat unpredictable in this city. Temperatures can reach up to 40 degrees on occasion.

Gran Malecon of Barranquilla...

Barranquilla's Great Malecón is a must-visit destination. You'll enjoy incredible ocean views and explore a stretch of coastline with regional culinary offerings and other attractions. The Malecón is also often a venue for regional cultural performances or important musical events like BarranquiJazz.

Barranquilla's Culinary...

Colombian food is delicious. You can try fried foods, all kinds of soups, fried fish, coconut rice, chicken rice, and much more. Also, salchipapa, a fast food dish made with potatoes, chorizo, and delicious sauces, is very popular in Barranquilla.
